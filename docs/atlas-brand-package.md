# Atlas Brand Package v1

Atlas is a strategy intelligence and performance attribution platform for traders.

## Positioning

Atlas helps traders understand whether a strategy works, why performance changes, where edge is created or destroyed, and how costs, expectancy, R capture, and behavior affect outcomes.

Atlas is not a signal service, broker, trading social network, or hype-driven retail trading product.

## Archetype

The Analyst + The Navigator

## Personality

- Intelligent
- Calm
- Analytical
- Institutional
- Precise
- Modern
- Trustworthy
- Minimalist

## Primary Message

Strategy Intelligence for Traders

## One-Sentence Description

Atlas helps traders understand why their strategy improves, degrades, or fails.

## Expanded Description

Atlas is a strategy intelligence platform that analyzes completed trades to identify hidden performance leaks, execution drag, expectancy deterioration, R capture problems, and strategy iteration changes.

### Logo Concept

The Atlas mark combines a geometric A, a compass bearing, and an ascending terrain peak. It communicates navigation, precision, elevation, and strategic perspective.

Avoid candlesticks, rockets, bulls, bears, dollar signs, and generic trading imagery.

## UI Principles

1. Show the most important insight first.
2. Reduce dashboard clutter.
3. Use progressive disclosure.
4. Prioritize readability over decoration.
5. Make workflows feel analytical, not gamified.

## Voice

Clear, objective, precise, and professional.

Example:

“Atlas classifies this report as Cost Drag Problem. Gross performance is positive, but execution costs are materially reducing realized profitability.”

Avoid:

- hype language
- guaranteed profitability
- social proof claims
- signal-style recommendations

## Website Direction

The Atlas website should feel like an institutional strategy lab:

- dark navy-black background
- restrained blue/cyan accents
- geometric data visuals
- strong first-screen diagnosis
- concise product explanation
- no decorative trading clichés
